Pasta Carbonara

The perfect 15 minute pasta dish!

Pasta Carbonara is a quick, yet delicious meal to toss together on a day when you don't have all the time in the world for cooking. Maybe not the healthiest dish you could eat, but damn is it delicious. And when making the Pasta Carbonara รก la JuarezTM, you won't care about healthy until you finish it.

Did you know that Pasta Carbonara was created by the vikings around year 5000 B.C? Many believe that this is an italian dish, but originally it is actually from Scandinavia. Here at Juarez INC we still make Pasta Carbonara the traditional way, just like our ancestors. You can read more about the history of Pasta Carbonara here!

Ingredients

1 serving

  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 dl of grated pecorino/parmiggano/grana padano etc
  • Lots of ground black pepper
  • 1 pack of bacon (ca 140 g)
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 125 g spagetti
  • Optional: 1 fresh red chili

Steps

  1. Whisk together the egg and egg yolk in a bowl. Save the egg white for your next day breakfast scrambled eggs, or give it to your dog
  2. Grate the cheese, and add it to the bowl
  3. Grind a lot of black pepper into the bowl, and whisk
  4. Chop up the onion and optional chili, and put to the side
  5. Chop up the bacon and add to a cold pan, put the heat on and let it warm up with the bacon in the pan
  6. Cook the pasta at the same time
  7. When bacon looks 90% done, add in your chopped onion and optional red chili
  8. When pasta is done, move it from the pot directly to your pan. Turn off heat and mix it with contents of the pan
  9. This is the tricky part. Have a pasta bowl ready. Pour the egg-mix onto the pasta and bacon in the pan and mix quickly. If too slow, you will get scrambled eggs. Pour it all in the pasta bowl
  10. Add more ground pepper if needed, as well as a bit more ground cheese
  11. Enjoy your Pasta Carbonara รก la JuarezTM
